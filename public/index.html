Short instruction

Before you are going to use (and love) our brand new digital assistant, we will give you some instructions.

  1. Our assistant is an english speaking guy, so please write english with him :)
  2. Click on the icon on the bottom right to start
  3. You always can restart the tutorial with the words "start tutorial"
  4. Please be friendly to him :)
  5. Have fun!
